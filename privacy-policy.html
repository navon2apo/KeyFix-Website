Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

Introduction

KeyFix is designed with security and user privacy in mind. This policy explains what information KeyFix collects and how it's used.

Data Collection

KeyFix is committed to minimal data collection:

  • Keyboard Layout Fixing: All text processing for keyboard layout fixing happens entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers.
  • Translation: When you use the translation feature, the selected text is sent to our translation provider to perform the translation.
  • Optional Feedback: If you submit feedback through the extension, this information along with your optional email address is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

KeyFix stores minimal data on your device:

  • Language preferences for translation
  • Extension settings
  • Submitted feedback (if any)

This data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and is not synchronized or transmitted elsewhere.

Third-Party Services

KeyFix uses the following third-party services:

  • Translation Services: Text submitted for translation is processed through external translation APIs. Only the text you explicitly select for translation is sent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository or by using the feedback form within the extension.
